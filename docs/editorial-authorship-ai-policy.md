# Editorial, Authorship, and AI Policy

## People-First Rule

Content must answer real buyer and engineer questions before it targets keywords. Pages should clarify what the equipment is, what it tests, which standards or methods it may relate to, what configuration affects suitability, and what the buyer should confirm before procurement.

## Who, How, and Why

Important technical pages should record:

```text
Prepared by
Technical reviewer
Standards reviewer
Last reviewed
Source basis
AI assistance note
```

Do not expose confidential internal evidence, but make the review basis clear enough for overseas B2B buyers to trust the page.

## AI Use

AI may help with:

- summarizing approved sources;
- drafting page briefs;
- improving English readability;
- creating FAQ drafts;
- proposing internal links;
- creating clearly labeled concept visuals.

AI must not:

- invent technical parameters;
- invent standard versions or clauses;
- claim certification or compliance;
- approve its own output;
- turn competitor information into KingPo facts;
- create mass pages without human review and unique value.
