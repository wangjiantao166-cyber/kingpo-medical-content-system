# Homepage Authority Hub

## Use When

Use this skill when designing or refreshing the homepage.

## Goal

Make the homepage a clear authority hub, not a random collection of links or marketing blocks.

## Required Homepage Sections

- Clear company positioning
- Core product category entrances
- Priority product or solution highlights
- Application scenario entrances
- Standards / compliance-related entrance
- Technical resources or latest articles
- FAQ or buyer guidance
- Inquiry CTA

## Link Strategy

Homepage should link to:

- Highest-priority L1 category pages
- High-value application pages
- Standards hub
- Technical resources hub
- Contact/inquiry page

Homepage should not link to every product model.

## Content Rules

Homepage copy should explain what KingPo does across test equipment categories, while allowing the medical-focused system to have its own strong cluster.

Do not describe KingPo as only a medical test equipment company.

## Coordination

Work with:

- `seo-site-architecture-planner` for L1 priorities
- `navigation-mega-menu-architect` for header/footer consistency
- `content-cluster-planner` for cluster selection
- `google-seo-eeat-content` for homepage content quality
- `inquiry-conversion` for CTA design

## Prohibited

- Do not overload homepage with excessive links.
- Do not keyword-stuff headings.
- Do not use unsupported claims or fake authority signals.
