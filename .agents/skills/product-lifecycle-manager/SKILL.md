# Product Lifecycle Manager

## Use When

Use this skill before drafting, publishing, updating, or retiring a product or R&D direction.

## Product Status Values

- Existing product
- Custom development available
- R&D direction
- R&D reference / needs confirmation
- Internal only
- Not public
- Legacy product
- Discontinued

## Status Effects

Existing products may include confirmed model, parameters, images, PDFs, and inquiry CTA.

Custom development pages may discuss test objects, requirements, configuration questions, and development evaluation. Avoid stock or delivery claims.

R&D directions may discuss direction, scenario, and technical questions. They must show confirmation status.

Internal-only and not-public items must not appear in public navigation, sitemap, or downloads.

Legacy/discontinued products require a keep, noindex, merge, redirect, or retire decision after URL and SEO review.

## Output

- Product status
- Allowed public wording
- Prohibited wording
- CTA recommendation
- Index/noindex recommendation
- Approval requirement

## Prohibited

- Do not upgrade product status without confirmed evidence.
- Do not publish internal-only or not-public items.
- Do not delete legacy pages without a URL plan.
