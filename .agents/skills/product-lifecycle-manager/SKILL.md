# Product Lifecycle Manager

## Use When

Use this skill before drafting, publishing, updating, or retiring a product or R&D direction.

## Product Status Values

- Existing product
- Custom development available
- R&D direction
- R&D reference / needs confirmation
- Internal only
- Not public
- Legacy product
- Discontinued

## Status Effects

### Existing Product

May include confirmed model, parameters, images, PDFs, and inquiry CTA.

### Custom Development Available

May discuss test objects, requirements, configuration questions, and development evaluation. Avoid stock or delivery claims.

### R&D Direction

May discuss direction, scenario, and technical questions. Must show confirmation status.

### R&D Reference / Needs Confirmation

Should stay draft or internal unless approved. Do not index publicly without review.

### Internal Only / Not Public

Do not expose in public pages, navigation, XML sitemap, or downloads.

### Legacy / Discontinued

Recommend keep, noindex, merge, redirect, or retire only after URL and SEO review.

## Workflow

1. Read product record and sources.
2. Identify evidence for current status.
3. Assign status.
4. Define allowed wording.
5. Define CTA type.
6. Define indexability.
7. Define review requirements.

## Output

- Product status
- Allowed public wording
- Prohibited wording
- CTA recommendation
- Index/noindex recommendation
- Approval requirement

## Prohibited

- Do not upgrade product status without confirmed evidence.
- Do not publish internal-only or not-public items.
- Do not delete legacy pages without a URL plan.
