# Orphan Link Auditor

## Use When

Use this skill after publishing pages, after importing products, before launch, and during monthly/quarterly SEO maintenance.

## Goal

Find pages that search engines or users cannot easily discover, and identify over-optimization or broken-link risks.

## Checks

- Orphan pages
- Pages with no meaningful outbound links
- Pages with no parent category
- Broken internal links
- Repeated exact-match anchors
- Too many links from one page
- Important pages buried too deeply
- Product pages not linked from category pages
- Articles that do not link back to pillar pages
- Standard pages with no related products

## Output

- Orphan page list
- Broken link list
- Weak page list
- Over-optimized anchor list
- Recommended parent page
- Recommended inbound links
- Recommended outbound links
- Priority level

## Coordination

Work with `internal-linking-architect`, `content-cluster-planner`, `seo-site-architecture-planner`, and `content-refresh-auditor`.

## Prohibited

- Do not auto-add links without relevance review.
- Do not create spammy sitewide links.
- Do not link to pages that should remain internal or noindex.
