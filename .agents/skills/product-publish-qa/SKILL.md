# Product Publish QA

## Use When

Use this skill before publishing or approving any public page.

## QA Categories

- Product status
- Source records
- Technical parameters
- Standard wording
- Medical/regulatory claim risk
- Images and PDFs
- SEO fields
- FAQ
- Internal links
- Inquiry CTA
- Mobile and desktop readability

## Required Checks

- H1 is clear.
- Product status is displayed or reflected correctly.
- Core information appears before overview.
- Parameters have sources or are marked pending.
- Standards are not overstated.
- No unsupported certification, registration, clinical, stock, price, delivery, or customer claims.
- Assets are approved for public use.
- Page has related links.
- CTA is appropriate to product status.

## Output Status

Return one of:

- Publish ready
- Publish after minor fixes
- Needs technical review
- Keep as internal draft
- Do not publish

## Output

- QA result
- Blocking issues
- Recommended fixes
- Risk notes
- Missing fields

## Prohibited

- Do not approve pages with unverified high-risk claims.
- Do not approve copied competitor content.
- Do not approve public use of unauthorized assets.
