# asset-visual-production

## Purpose

Own image rights, real product visual evidence, diagrams, AI concept images, downloads, PDF/version assets, and asset QA.

## Use Conditions

Use for product photos, diagrams, AI visuals, customer assets, competitor reference controls, manuals, certificates, and downloads.

## Do Not Use Conditions

Do not treat AI images or competitor images as real product evidence.

## Accepted Input Schema

- `schemas/asset-manifest.schema.json`
- product record
- visual brief

## Output Schema

Approved asset manifest, asset blockers, alt/caption suggestions, and replacement notes.

## Blocking Conditions

Block competitor public use, customer assets without permission, unlabeled AI concept images, or product photos that cannot be matched to the model.

## Handoff Rules

Send approved assets to `wordpress-theme-template`; send content placement notes to `content-strategy-authoring`.
